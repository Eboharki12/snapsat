1. Landsat specifies the wavelengths of light over which it captures images as a series of bands.

    • LXSPPPRRRYYYYDDDGSIVV
    • L = Landsat
    • X = Sensor
    • S= Satellite
    • PPP = WRS path
    • RRR = WRS row
    • YYYY = Year
    • DDD = Julian day of year
    • GSI = Ground station identifier
    • VV = Archive version number
